## Winning Formula

### Before Demo
1. Practice 10+ times
2. Time yourself (stay under 10 min)
3. Prepare for 20 questions
4. Test all backups

### During Demo
1. Tell a story (patient impact)
2. Show, don't tell (live demo)
3. Emphasize innovation
4. Prove sustainability

### During Q&A
1. Listen completely
2. Answer directly
3. Circle back to impact
4. Show confidence, not arrogance

### After Demo
1. Thank judges
2. Network with other teams
3. Learn from feedback
4. Stay positive
