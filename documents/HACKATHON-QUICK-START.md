## ğŸš¨ Emergency Protocols

### **If Demo Crashes**
1. Don't panic (stay calm)
2. Switch to backup video immediately
3. Say: "Let me show you the backup recording"
4. Continue narrating over video
5. Explain: "This is the actual working platform"

### **If Projector Fails**
1. Offer to present on laptop screen
2. Judges gather around
3. More intimate demo (can be good!)

### **If You Forget Something**
1. Pause, take 2 seconds
2. Don't say "um" repeatedly
3. Bridge: "Let me show you why this matters..."
4. Continue with next point

### **If Tough Question**
1. Listen fully (don't interrupt)
2. If you know: Answer directly
3. If you don't: "Great question. We haven't solved that yet, but here's how we'd approach it..."
4. Never lie or make up answers
