## ðŸŽ¨ Design Guidelines (Apply to All Slides)

### Color Palette
```
Primary: #2196F3 (Medical Blue)
Secondary: #4CAF50 (Success Green)
Accent: #FF9800 (Alert Orange)
Text: #333333 (Dark Gray)
Background: #FFFFFF (White) or #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)
Dark Mode: #1F1E0B (for contrast)
```

### Typography
- **Headings**: Montserrat Bold, 40-60pt
- **Subheadings**: Montserrat Semibold, 24-32pt
- **Body**: Roboto Regular, 18-24pt
- **Small Text**: Roboto Light, 14-16pt

### Layout Principles
1. **One idea per slide** (no clutter)
2. **Large fonts** (readable from back of room)
3. **Minimal text** (speak it, don't write it)
4. **High-quality images** (no pixelated stock photos)
5. **Consistent branding** (logos, colors throughout)

### Visual Hierarchy
- Important numbers: **LARGE and BOLD**
- Supporting text: Medium
- Details: Small (or better, omit)

## ðŸš¨ Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Too much text on slides**
   - Solution: One idea per slide, large fonts

2. **Technical jargon without explanation**
   - Solution: Explain in simple terms

3. **Weak problem statement**
   - Solution: Use big numbers, real stories

4. **No live demo**
   - Solution: Always demo, have backup video

5. **Vague "future plans"**
   - Solution: Specific timeline, costs, metrics

6. **Ignoring business model**
   - Solution: Clear pricing, revenue projections

7. **"Everyone" as target market**
   - Solution: Specific segments (PSD, Hospital, NGO)
