## Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Medical blue (#2196F3)
- **Secondary**: Success green (#4CAF50)
- **Accent**: Warning orange (#FF9800)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#333) on light, White on dark
- **Background**: Clean white or subtle blue gradient

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins)
- **Body**: Clean, readable (Roboto, Open Sans)
- **Size**: Large enough to read from back of room

### Imagery
- High-quality, relevant images
- Avoid stock photo clichÃ©s
- Use real screenshots from platform
- Minimize text on slides (speak it instead)

### Animations
- Minimal, professional
- Fade or slide transitions only
- No spinning or flying text
- Keep it classy

## Presentation Tips

### Before You Start
- Arrive 15 minutes early
- Test projector/screen
- Have backup (USB, cloud, laptop)
- Breathe, relax

### During Presentation
- Make eye contact with judges
- Speak clearly and slowly
- Use gestures but don't pace
- Smile and show passion
- Watch the time

### Handling Q&A
- Listen fully before answering
- Repeat question if needed
- Answer directly, then elaborate
- "Great question!" (buy time to think)
- Admit if you don't know, but explain how you'd find out
